About Kaitlyn

Hi! I'm Kaitlyn DeBough, the photographer behind Corsco Camera. Based in Tucson, Arizona, I specialize in pet and portrait photography that captures the unique personality and spirit of every subject.

With over 5 years of professional photography experience, I've had the joy of working with countless pets and their families. What started as a passion has blossomed into my life's work, and I couldn't be more grateful to do what I love every single day.

I work with professional-grade equipment to ensure every photo is crisp, beautiful, and truly captures the moment. Whether it's the playful energy of a puppy, the gentle wisdom of a senior pet, or a candid family moment, I'm here to preserve your memories.
